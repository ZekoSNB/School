## Krátenie:
### 1
$$
{15^{/5} \over 20^{/5} } = {3 \over 4}
$$
### 2
$$
x^{11} \neq 0
$$
$$
{21x^7 \over 7x^{11}} = {3x^7 \over x^{11}} = 3x^{-4}
$$
### 3
- $x \neq 0$
$$
{12x^2-2xy \over 16x^2} = {2x(6x-y) \over 16x^2} = {1(6x-y) \over 8x } = {6x-y \over 8x}
$$
### 4
- $a \neq 0 \space or \space b \neq 0$
$$
{b+b^2 \over a+ab} = {b (1+b) \over a(1 + b)} = {b\over a}
$$
